General Questions

PropGo.my is Malaysia's AI-enhanced real estate platform designed specifically for property professionals including agents, lawyers, and bankers. Our platform streamlines property transactions through intelligent tools, integrated workflows, and AI-powered features that help you close deals faster and provide better service to your clients.

Unlike traditional property listing sites, PropGo.my focuses on the B2B aspect of real estate transactions. We provide a comprehensive ecosystem that connects all professionals involved in property deals. Our platform features AI-powered tools for content generation, integrated workflows between agents, lawyers and bankers, and a unique WTB/WTR (Want to Buy/Want to Rent) request system that helps match clients with the right properties.

PropGo.my is designed for real estate professionals in Malaysia, including:

  • Real estate agents and agencies
  • Property lawyers and legal firms
  • Mortgage bankers and financial institutions
  • Property developers and management companies

We offer different subscription plans tailored to each professional's needs.

Yes, PropGo.my is available nationwide across Malaysia. Our platform supports property transactions in all states and major cities including Kuala Lumpur, Penang, Johor Bahru, Ipoh, Kuching, Kota Kinabalu, and more. We continuously update our location database to ensure comprehensive coverage across the country.

AI Tools

Our AI listing generator uses advanced computer vision and natural language processing to create professional property listings in minutes. Simply upload property photos and provide the address, and our AI will:

  • Identify key property features from images (rooms, amenities, condition)
  • Generate compelling property descriptions
  • Extract and verify location details
  • Suggest optimal pricing based on market data
  • Format everything into a professional listing ready to publish

You can review and edit the generated content before publishing.

Our one-click marketing tool can generate a variety of promotional materials:

  • Digital property flyers and brochures
  • Social media posts optimized for different platforms
  • WhatsApp and SMS property descriptions
  • Email marketing templates
  • Property highlight videos (Pro plan)
  • Virtual tour scripts (Pro plan)

All generated content maintains your branding and can be customized to your preferences.

Our AI-generated legal documents are created based on Malaysian legal templates and standards. While they provide a solid foundation for agreements, we recommend having a qualified lawyer review them before finalizing. The documents are designed to be legally compliant, but specific circumstances may require customization.

For Law Firm subscribers, our platform allows lawyers to easily review and modify the AI-generated documents within the system, add their professional input, and then share them with clients.

Our AI neighborhood scoring system analyzes multiple data points to provide accurate insights:

  • Public transportation proximity and options
  • School quality and accessibility
  • Crime statistics from official sources
  • Amenities density (shops, restaurants, parks)
  • Historical property value trends
  • Development plans and future infrastructure

The system is continuously updated with new data and has been validated against expert assessments with an accuracy rate of over 85%.

Account & Billing

PropGo.my offers several subscription tiers:

  • Free: 5 listings per agent, 3 AI documents per month, basic dashboard access
  • Agent Basic (RM29/month): 20 listings per agent, WTB/WTR reply access, 10 AI documents per month, basic CRM features
  • Agent Pro (RM69/month): Unlimited listings, full AI tools access, marketing content generation, advanced dashboard, priority support
  • Law/Bank Firm (RM99/month): Staff access management, AI contract tools, pipeline integration, document management, client portal access

Enterprise plans with custom features are also available for larger agencies and firms.

You can easily change your subscription plan at any time:

  1. Log in to your PropGo.my account
  2. Go to "Account Settings" in the top-right menu
  3. Select "Subscription & Billing"
  4. Click "Change Plan" and select your desired plan
  5. Confirm your selection

When upgrading, the new features will be available immediately, and you'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle. When downgrading, the changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

PropGo.my accepts various payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • Online Banking (FPX)
  • E-wallets (Touch 'n Go, GrabPay, Boost)
  • Bank Transfer (for annual subscriptions)

All transactions are processed securely through our payment partner, Stripe. For enterprise plans, we also offer invoice-based payments.

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for new subscribers. If you're not satisfied with our service within the first 14 days, you can request a full refund by contacting our support team.

For subscription cancellations after the 14-day period, we don't provide prorated refunds, but you can continue using the service until the end of your current billing cycle.

Please refer to our Refund Policy for more details.
